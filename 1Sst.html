The Bauer Supreme 1S hockey stick is a mid kick point stick designed for fully loaded and powerful shots.

The transfer of energy from the handle to the blade is maximized using Maximum Power Kick (MPK) Technology. This ensures that during full motion shots, all the power that you put into the stick explodes through the puck.

The kick point of the stick is higher up on the shaft than other sticks due to the reinforced taper that is coser to the blade. This increases duability which allows for a more powerful flex for more powerful shots.

The stick's unique gold-weave TeXtreme carbon fiber is 20% lighter than other carbon fiber but with the same durability. This results in a faster wind-up and a more explosive follow through for even harder shots.

Pucks, slashes, and skates cam all cause tiny cracks in your stick, but Bauer's eLASTech Technology makes sure to stop the spread of these fractures, allowing your stick to perform to the best of its ability all the time.
